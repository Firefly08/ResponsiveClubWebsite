ABOUT US

Immerse yourself in the world of the Blues by becoming a Fan Club member today. With membership, you'll have access to:

  • A huge library of football video content including full match highlights, behind-the-scenes footage, exclusive interviews and features
  • The latest clubs news and happenings
  • The club's official fan newsletter and emails most relevant to you
  • Regular competitions exclusively for Fan Club members
  • Link up with the nearest supporters fan club wherever you are
  • Interact with fans around the city, share your thoughts and discuss all the goings on in our cosy location
